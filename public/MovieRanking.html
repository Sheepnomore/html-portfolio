The Best Movie According to Lo

My top 5 Movie of all-time

'Celine and Julie Go Boating' Jacques Rivette

This is the first movie that has ever touched upon my soul.

'Nostalgia' Andrei Tarkovsky

I love it for its topic addresses my keen interest of the "Motherland"

'The Suspended Step of the Stork' Thodōros Angelopoulos

I love it because it express a certain nostalgia toward the situation, instead of a direct representation.

'Drive My Car' Ryusuke Hamaguchi

It's the greatest text-based visual poem I have ever seen.

'Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind' Hayao Miyazaki

No further excuses, this is one of the best anime movie from Hayao Miyazaki.
